Research Design | 研究设计

Interpretivist Philosophy | 解释主义哲学

Understanding complex, context-dependent phenomena through human interpretation and meaning construction 通过人类解释和意义建构理解复杂、情境依赖的社会现象
Incorporating critical realism: structural conditions shape but don't determine actor behavior 融入批判实在论：结构性条件塑造但不决定行动者行为
Qualitative Case Study | 定性案例研究
Critical Case Selection (Flyvbjerg, 2006): KL represents most favorable conditions for observing mechanisms 关键案例选择：吉隆坡代表观察互动机制的最有利条件
Why KL? (1) Global Islamic finance leadership; (2) Active BRI participation; (3) Clear intersection observable 为何选择吉隆坡？全球伊斯兰金融领导地位 + 积极的一带一路参与 + 清晰的交叉点

Process Tracing | 过程追踪

Tracing causal mechanisms linking causes to outcomes (Beach & Pedersen, 2013) 追踪因果机制，连接原因与结果，揭示"如何"与"为什么"

Comparative Design | 比较设计

Pattern matching with Hong Kong (strategic window) and Dubai (market-driven) to identify KL's uniqueness 与香港（战略窗口模式）和迪拜（市场驱动模式）进行模式匹配，识别吉隆坡独特性

Data Collection | 数据收集

15-20 semi-structured interviews

官方文件、监管报告

Main Case | 主案例

ECRL SDG Sukuk: First government-guaranteed SDG Sukuk in Malaysia, representing flagship BRI project financing 东海岸铁路可持续发展目标伊斯兰债券：马来西亚首个政府担保SDG Sukuk，代表一带一路旗舰项目融资

Data Analysis | 数据分析

Four-Stage Analysis | 四阶段分析

1 Data Preparation | 数据准备
Transcription, filing, organization of raw data
转录、归档、原始数据组织
2 Dimensional Coding | 维度编码
Using S-I-M framework (NVivo software)
基于战略-制度-市场三维框架（使用NVivo）
3 Cross-dimensional Analysis | 跨维度分析
Identifying interactions between dimensions
识别维度间的互动关系
4 Synthesis & Comparison | 综合与比较
Comparison with Hong Kong & Dubai cases
与香港及迪拜案例进行比较分析

Audit trail documentation
