Islamic Finance Instruments Spectrum

Kuala Lumpur Financial Hub Internationalization Analysis

Sukuk (Islamic Bonds)

Asset-backed securities structured to comply with Shariah principles, avoiding interest (riba).

Mudarabah

Profit-sharing partnership where one party provides capital and the other provides expertise.

Musharakah

Joint venture structure with profit and loss sharing based on capital contribution ratios.

Murabaha

Cost-plus financing arrangement where the bank purchases the asset and sells it at a marked-up price.
