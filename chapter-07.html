说明 NOTE
ECRL SDG Sukuk是研究的"皇冠案例"，其风险分担机制（政治风险、完工风险、运营风险）的分配逻辑、可持续发展目标的嵌入方式、交易过程中的关键决策点，必须依靠参与者的第一手叙述。没有访谈数据，本章节内容将沦为二手资料的简单复述。
The ECRL SDG Sukuk is the "crown jewel" case of this research. The allocation logic of its risk-sharing mechanisms (political, completion, and operational risks), the embedding of sustainable development goals, and key decision points in the transaction process must rely on first-hand narratives from participants. Without interview data, this chapter would merely replicate secondary sources.
