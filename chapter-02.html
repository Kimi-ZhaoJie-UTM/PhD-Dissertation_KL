Islamic Finance Theory | 伊斯兰金融理论

Core Tension | 核心张力

Form-compliance vs Essence-compliance (El-Gamal, 2006; Al-Jarhi, 2017) 形式合规 vs 本质合规：部分产品形式上满足教法要求，但实质上接近传统债务工具
Commercial viability vs Religious authenticity tension throughout historical evolution 商业可行性与宗教真实性之间的贯穿性张力

Key Instruments | 核心金融工具谱系

Murabaha (成本加价): Cost-plus financing, most common but criticized as "debt-like" 成本加价融资，最常用但被批评类似债务
Ijara (租赁): Leasing-based, closer to Islamic finance essence 租赁融资，更接近伊斯兰金融本质
Mudaraba/Musharaka (合伙): Profit-sharing partnerships, essence-compliant but complex 利润分享合伙，符合本质但结构复杂
Sukuk (伊斯兰债券): Asset-backed securities, key innovation for capital markets 资产支持证券，资本市场关键创新

Malaysian Model | 马来西亚模式

Dual Banking System: Conventional and Islamic banks operating in parallel with unified regulatory framework 双银行体系：传统与伊斯兰银行并行运作，统一监管框架
Centralized SAC: Shariah Advisory Council providing binding rulings for entire industry 集中化教法委员会：为全行业提供具有约束力的教法裁决
Embedded in NEP: Linked to Bumiputera economic empowerment policy, creating political legitimacy 嵌入新经济政策：与土著经济政策关联，创造政治合法性

3D Framework | 三维互动框架

Framework Dimensions | 框架维度

Strategic Alignment Layer | 战略协同层
National policy coordination, bilateral agreements, political will, strategic positioning
国家政策协调、双边协议、政治意愿、战略定位
Institutional Coordination Layer | 制度协同层
Regulatory frameworks, Shariah governance, legal infrastructure, professional talent
监管框架、教法治理、法律基础设施、专业人才
Market Integration Layer | 市场融合层
Product innovation, capital flows, institutional partnerships, investor networks
产品创新、资本流动、机构合作、投资者网络

Interaction Logics | 互动逻辑

Top-down Empowerment | 自上而下赋能
Strategic commitment enables institutional development and market activities
战略承诺推动制度发展和市场活动
Bottom-up Feedback | 自下而上反馈
Market success reinforces strategic commitment and institutional investment
市场成功强化战略承诺和制度投资
Horizontal Spillover | 横向溢出
Developments within one dimension create lateral effects
同一维度内的发展产生横向效应
Recursive Reinforcement | 递归强化
Cross-dimensional interactions create self-reinforcing dynamics
跨维度互动创造自我强化的动态机制

BRI Financial Cooperation | 一带一路金融合作

Policy banks (CDB, Exim Bank), AIIB, Silk Road Fund, and the need for diversified financing channels. 政策性银行（国开行、进出口银行）、亚投行、丝路基金，以及融资渠道多元化的需求。

Gaps & Theory | 空白与理论

Discursive Fragmentation | 话语割裂
Islamic finance and BRI studies operate in parallel with limited cross-fertilization
伊斯兰金融与一带一路研究平行运行，缺乏交叉融合
Single-Level Analysis | 单一层次分析
Lack of meso-level framework connecting macro-strategy, meso-institution, and micro-market
缺乏连接宏观战略、中观制度与微观市场的中观框架
Case Study Deficit | 案例聚焦不足
Limited systematic case study on KL's pathway as specialized IFC interacting with BRI
缺乏将吉隆坡作为专业金融中心与一带一路互动的系统案例研究
Mechanism Identification | 机制识别有限
Describing existence of interaction rather than explaining causal mechanisms
描述互动的存在而非解释因果机制
IFC Theory | 金融中心理论
Specialization strategy (Poon et al., 2016), Path dependence (DiMaggio & Powell, 1983), Frontier position
专业化战略、路径依赖、前沿定位理论
Developmental State | 发展型国家
State-led industrial policy approach in financial sector (Lai, 2015; Rethel, 2021)
金融领域国家主导的产业政策方法
