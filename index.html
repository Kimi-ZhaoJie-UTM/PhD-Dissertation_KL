Core Concepts | 核心概念界定

Islamic Finance | 伊斯兰金融

Financial system adhering to Shariah principles: prohibition of Riba (interest), Gharar (excessive uncertainty), emphasis on risk-sharing, asset-backing, and ethical investment. 遵循伊斯兰教法原则的金融体系：禁止利息与过度不确定性，强调风险分担、资产支持与道德投资。

Belt and Road Initiative | 一带一路倡议

Transnational infrastructure cooperation framework proposed by China in 2013, covering policy coordination, facilities connectivity, unimpeded trade, financial integration, and people-to-people bonds. 2013年中国提出的跨国基础设施与发展合作框架，涵盖政策沟通、设施联通、贸易畅通、资金融通、民心相通五大领域。

International Financial Center | 国际金融中心

Global nodes concentrating financial institutions, transactions, and talents. KL represents the "specialized" type with Islamic finance as core competency. 聚集金融机构、交易与人才的全球节点。吉隆坡代表"专业型"金融中心，以伊斯兰金融为核心竞争力。

Interaction Mechanism | 互动机制

Processes of mutual influence at strategic, institutional, and market levels, with dynamic bidirectional interactions (top-down empowerment, bottom-up feedback, horizontal spillover, recursive reinforcement). 战略、制度、市场三个层面上的相互影响过程，具有动态双向互动特征（自上而下赋能、自下而上反馈、横向溢出、递归强化）。

Significance Summary | 意义总结

Theoretical: Proposing 3D framework, enriching IFC theory, advancing South-South finance research 理论：提出三维框架，丰富金融中心理论，推进南南金融研究
Practical: Policy guidance for Malaysia, China, and other BRI countries 实践：为马来西亚、中国及其他一带一路国家提供政策指导

Interaction Logics | 互动逻辑

Top-down Empowerment: Strategic commitment enables institutional development and market activities 自上而下赋能：战略承诺推动制度发展和市场活动
Bottom-up Feedback: Market success reinforces strategic commitment and institutional investment 自下而上反馈：市场成功强化战略承诺和制度投资
Horizontal Spillover: Developments within one dimension create lateral effects 横向溢出：同一维度内的发展产生横向效应
Recursive Reinforcement: Cross-dimensional interactions create self-reinforcing dynamics 递归强化：跨维度互动创造自我强化的动态机制

Chapter Seven [Core] | 第七章【重点】

Case Study: ECRL SDG Sukuk | 案例深描：东海岸铁路伊斯兰债券

Transaction Structure | 交易结构

ECRL Project | 东海岸铁路项目

665km railway connecting Kota Bharu to Port Klang, RM44 billion (renegotiated), largest BRI investment in Southeast Asia 665公里连接哥打巴鲁至巴生港，440亿令吉（重新谈判后），东南亚最大一带一路投资

Sukuk Structure | 债券结构

Ijarah (lease-based) with hybrid elements, Sovereign guarantee from Malaysian Government, First government-guaranteed SDG Sukuk 租赁型含混合元素，马来西亚政府主权担保，首个政府担保SDG伊斯兰债券

Financing Architecture | 融资架构

Multi-tranche structure with ESG-linked pricing mechanism, demonstrating innovative blended finance approach 多批次结构与ESG挂钩定价机制，展示创新混合融资方法

Risk Sharing | 风险分担机制

Chapter Eight | 第八章

Comparative Analysis & Synthesis | 比较分析与机制综合

Hong Kong | 香港比较

"Strategic Window" Model | "战略窗口"模式

Government-led but limited institutional depth; focus on sovereign Sukuk issuance (2014-2017) 政府主导但制度深度有限；专注于主权伊斯兰债券发行（2014-2017）

Key Differences | 关键差异

No dual banking system, limited Shariah scholar networks, smaller domestic market, focus on RMB-denominated Sukuk 无双银行体系、教法学者网络有限、国内市场较小、专注人民币计价债券

Limitations | 局限性

Lack of organic Islamic finance ecosystem, reliance on external Shariah advisory, no indigenous talent pipeline 缺乏有机伊斯兰金融生态系统、依赖外部教法咨询、无本土人才管道

Dubai | 迪拜比较

"Market-Driven" Model | "市场驱动"模式

DIFC-based, sovereign wealth fund driven, competitive but less coordinated than Malaysia's state-led approach 基于迪拜国际金融中心、主权财富基金驱动，竞争性强但协调度低于马来西亚国家主导模式

Key Differences | 关键差异

Less state intervention, GCC-centric transactions, no systematic BRI integration, decentralized Shariah governance 较少国家干预、海湾中心交易、无系统一带一路整合、分散化教法治理

Strengths | 优势

Strong wealth fund backing, international legal framework (English common law), tax efficiency 强大的财富基金支持、国际法律框架（英国普通法）、税收效率

Mechanism Synthesis | 机制综合

KL Uniqueness | 吉隆坡独特性

Deep Specialization + State Leadership (unlike HK's shallow specialization or Dubai's market-driven approach) 深度专业化 + 国家领导（不同于香港浅层专业化或迪拜市场驱动）
Institutional Embeddedness: 40 years of path-dependent development creating "soft infrastructure" 制度嵌入性：40年路径依赖发展创造"软基础设施"

Identified Mechanisms | 识别的机制

Recursive Institutionalization: Feedback loops between strategy, institutions, and market 递归制度化：战略、制度与市场间的反馈循环
Legitimation Transfer: BRI legitimacy enhances KL's Islamic finance credibility 合法性转移：一带一路合法性增强吉隆坡伊斯兰金融信誉
Network Centrality: KL as bridge between Middle East capital and Asian projects 网络中心性：吉隆坡作为中东资本与亚洲项目间的桥梁

Concluding Remark | 结语

KL's pathway demonstrates how specialized financial centers leverage strategic niche positioning and institutional depth to engage with transnational initiatives, creating recursive dynamics reinforcing global status. 吉隆坡的路径展示了专业金融中心如何通过战略利基定位和制度深度参与跨国发展倡议，创造递归动态以强化其全球地位。
