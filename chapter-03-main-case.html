Risk Allocation Mechanism

Risk Allocation Framework

Political Risk

Covered by government sovereign guarantee, ensuring principal safety for investors. Narrowest spread for government-guaranteed Sukuk in Malaysian history.

Completion Risk

China Communications Construction Company (CCCC) as contractor, assuming construction period risk through performance bonds. Project progress ahead of schedule at 86.07% completion.

Operational Risk

Shared between project company and government through operations agreement. Expected annual ridership of 5.6 million passengers, freight volume of 13.7 million tonnes.

SDG Compliance Risk

Third-party certification body oversight with continuous investor monitoring. Annual CO₂ reduction of 1 million tonnes, aligned with SDG 9 objectives.
