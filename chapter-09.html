说明 NOTE
第九章作为论文的结论章节，主要基于前文第四章至第八章的实证分析进行理论提炼与综合。重点在于回应绪论提出的研究问题，阐述"战略-制度-市场"三维框架的理论价值，并为马来西亚政策制定者、中国金融机构以及一带一路沿线国家提供循证建议。
As the concluding chapter, Chapter 9 focuses on theoretical synthesis based on empirical analysis from Chapters 4-8. It aims to respond to the research questions posed in the introduction, elaborate on the theoretical value of the "Strategy-Institution-Market" framework, and provide evidence-based recommendations for Malaysian policymakers, Chinese financial institutions, and BRI participating countries.
