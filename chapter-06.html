说明 NOTE
市场融合的具体机制——为何选择某种产品结构、如何吸引中东投资者、机构合作的谈判过程——需要从从业者的叙述中获取。市场数据（如发行量、定价）可佐证，但无法解释"为什么"和"如何"。
The specific mechanisms of market integration—why certain product structures were chosen, how Middle Eastern investors were attracted, the negotiation process of institutional cooperation—require narratives from practitioners. Market data (issuance volume, pricing) provides corroboration but cannot explain the "why" and "how".
